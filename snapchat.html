Features

Below are some Snapchat's features that will be further elaborated upon.

  1. Lenses
  2. Chat
  3. Geo Location

Lenses

While taking photos or videos, users are given the ability to apply "lenses." These lenses use facial recognition software to make digital changes to the user's face. This produces unique effects such as making the user's eyes or mouth larger. The lense feature has made Snapchat extremely popular with its user base.

Chat

One of Snapchat's main features is the chat feature. Here, users are able to contact each other through short texts, photos, or videos. Additionally, users are able to send personal "bitmojis," which are emoticons that the user has customized.

Geo Location

Perhaps one of the most controversial featurse of Snapchat is its "geo-location" feature. It allows users to hide or display their current location and view the current location of other users. Using a smartphone's hardware, Snapchat is able to accurately assume if users are in a car or sleeping.

My Experience

My experience with Snapchat has been tumultuous. In the past, I used SnapChat religiously; I would spend hours chatting with friends or posting on my story. After a while, my enthusiasm for Snapchat has mellowed down. Now, I rarely use SnapChat because I prefer to keep updated on my friends' lives through Instagram. I give Snapchat a rating of 2/5 because I mostly just use it for its lense feature.
